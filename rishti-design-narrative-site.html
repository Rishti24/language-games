Values

Presence

Design that invites attentive touch and sustained attention—measured, calm, and intentional.

Material Honesty

Respect the properties of materials—weight, grain, friction—so interactions feel grounded and reliable.

Emotional Craft

Small gestures tuned to human perception—soft timing, gentle relief, and quiet delight.

Material + Mechanism

Material

Layered textiles, warm ceramics, and soft polymers create a vocabulary of touch—contrasts of smooth and textured surfaces guide places to press, slide and cradle.

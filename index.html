Pastry Delights

Flaky, crisp, airy, creamy, chewy, or delicate — finished with fruit jams, custards, chocolate, or cream.

Almond-Dusted Morning Croissant

A golden croissant kissed with toasted almond sugar and filled with silky almond frangipane and a ribbon of apricot jam — perfect with morning coffee.

Nutella-Strawberry Pancake Stack

Fluffy mini pancakes layered with hazelnut-chocolate spread, macerated strawberries, and a swirl of whipped cream — a playful take on brunch classics.

Lavender-Honey Éclair

Choux pastry piped with a delicate lavender-infused honey custard, glazed with a thin ribbon of caramel — floral, sweet, and airy.

Pistachio-Rose Macaron

Crunchy-shelled macarons with a silky pistachio ganache and a whisper of rosewater cream — a fragrant, elegant bite.

Sticky Toffee Roll

A warm, soft roll swirled with brown sugar and toffee, finished with toasted pecans and a drizzle of salted caramel.

Citrus-Berry Danish

Buttery, layered Danish crowned with lemon curd and a scatter of fresh berries — bright, tart, and delicately flaky.

Featured Fillings & Flavours

  • Almond frangipane & apricot jam
  • Nutella, fresh strawberries, whipped cream
  • Lavender-honey custard & salted caramel
  • Pistachio ganache, rose cream, lemon curd

Pairing Tips

Match light, airy pastries with fruit or light creams; reserve dense, chewy pastries for rich fillings like caramel or chocolate.
